# Prompting Guide

## Base rule

Write one strong base description, then add slide-specific changes.

## Lock across all 6 slides

- subject identity
- camera angle
- room/body/layout structure
- major background elements
- lighting direction

## Change per slide

- style
- decor
- color treatment
- degree of transformation
- emotional tone

## Good base prompt shape

```text
iPhone photo of a [specific scene]. Shot from [specific angle].
[Specific room/person/object details].
Natural lighting, realistic phone camera quality, portrait orientation.
No text, no logos, no watermarks.
```

## Avoid

- "a nice living room"
- "beautiful person"
- changing the whole scene every slide
- forgetting portrait orientation

## Practical note

If the first batch looks too AI-generated, add more concrete physical details and fewer abstract adjectives.
